<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>连环画：走过劫难（1）</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-62-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>连环画：走过劫难（轩远工作室）
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="color: #800000;"><em>这个故事的每一细节都是真实的，逼真还原了中国北京的看守所、北京新安女子劳教所、北京女子监狱的场景及发生的事件，只是掩去了人物的真实姓名。</em></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff9900;">一、去了一次天安门，我就被劳教了</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11480731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-62.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11480731 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-62-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">1. 我是一个非常普通的女人，大学毕业后，我嫁了一个老实的丈夫，生了一个儿子。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480734" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2-24.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11480734 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2-24-600x436.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2. 我总是和儿子说，“要乖，听话。”家里老人经历过各种政治运动，经常告诉我：“不要参与政治。”那时我就想，听话，不惹事，无论什么世道，人都不会太惨吧。我小心翼翼地过着自己的日子，只求一生平平安安。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480745" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/3-27.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11480745 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/3-27-600x442.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="442" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">3. 在北京我有一个不错的工作，也很努力，很年轻我就晋升了单位的业务主管。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480752" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/4-15.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11480752 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/4-15-600x414.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">4. 谁能想到，不到30岁的我竟然查出肺癌，已经是晚期了，现代医学已无药可医。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480755" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/5-13.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11480755 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/5-13-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">5. 朋友来教我学<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md">法轮功</a>。求生的欲望让我还是想试一试。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480759" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/6-8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11480759 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/6-8-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">6. 奇迹的出现是我没有想到的，医生都震惊了，认为不可能活过半年的我，炼<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md">法轮功</a>后，不但病好了，竟然比以前还要健康年轻。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480765" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/7-7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11480765 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/7-7-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">7. 1999年10月，单位领导找我，说共产党员不许炼法轮功，法轮功是“邪教”。我确实是党员，因为当初想晋级就必须入党啊。我只想祛病健身，不想惹麻烦，他们说保证不炼功就行。于是我签了保证书。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480770" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/8-5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11480770 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/8-5-600x427.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">8. 为了自己的身体，我继续在家偷偷炼功。2001年10月，听说很多法轮功学员去天安门上访，但我一直不敢去。一天， 我带孩子经过天安门，就想过去看看。 广场上，警察都换了黑色制服，我看到一个拉横幅的孕妇被抓了，我很害怕正要走开，一个老大爷打坐的样子吸引了我，接着，警察和便衣把我拉开了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480772" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/9-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11480772 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/9-2-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">9. 警察问我：“你是炼法轮功的吗？”我小声地说“是”。我只是觉得不能说假话而已。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480778" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/10-10.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11480778 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/10-10-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">10. 警察马上就把我推进了警车。在警车里，一个小女孩被警察摔进来，她妈妈也被抓了，我紧紧抱住了我儿子。我什么都没做啊，我会被带到什么地方？</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11481639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/11-43.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11481639 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/11-43-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">11. 我被关进“法制教育学习班”，这是一个宾馆，但其实是非法囚禁人的“黑监狱”，保安整天看着我。单位领导也来了，每天大吃大喝，都是我们单位花钱。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11481640" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/12-4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11481640 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/12-4-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">12. 领导劝我为了前途，放弃法轮功，不要“参与政治”。如果我能深入“揭批”法轮功，就可以继续回单位上班。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11481642" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/13-6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11481642 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/13-6-600x385.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="385" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">13. 学习班的人逼我看CCTV录制的“天安门自焚”，这和我学的法轮功完全不是一回事啊。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11481646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/14-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11481646 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/14-3-600x476.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="476" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">14. 丈夫儿子都被叫来了。丈夫很无奈，他知道是法轮功师父救了我的命，他无法劝我。儿子哭起来：“妈妈，您听话，咱们一起回家吧。”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11481651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/15-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11481651 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/15-3-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">15. 人总不能陷害自己的救命恩人吧。我无法按他们的要求写“揭批”。警察来“接”我了。在宾馆门口，我听到儿子喊：“妈妈，您去哪啊？”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11481664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/16-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11481664 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/16-3-600x429.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="429" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">16. 我被送进了看守所，在门口，我被电棍顶着：“下蹲！低头抱首！”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11481669" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/17-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11481669 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/17-2-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">17. 刚进看守所的人，必须当众把衣服脱光检查。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11481671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/18-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11481671 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/18-3-600x379.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="379" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">18. 无论多冷，都要到风场“洗澡”， 她们对我还算客气了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11481672" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/19-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11481672 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/19-1-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">19. 我很难受。自己一向循规蹈矩，怎么就成了犯罪嫌疑人？怎么会和小偷、吸毒的、卖淫的、打架斗殴的关在一起？我5岁的儿子还从没有离开过妈妈啊。</figcaption></figure>
