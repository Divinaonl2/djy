<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>108年度交趾陶艺术椅装置艺术揭幕典礼</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8c6d942f056956379f83aaea4accdb21-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>世贤国小二张交趾陶艺术造型椅揭幕仪式。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月04日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E8%B4%A4%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md">世贤国小</a>校园内拥有5张交趾陶造型椅，3日揭幕的两张交趾陶造型椅，其中一张是“阿里山小火车”；另一张则是以嘉义人文管乐小鸡为意象的“日出台湾”。二张椅子都是结合在地文化的亮丽装置艺术。成为世贤国小美感校园新亮点。</p>
<figure id="11698192" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8c6d942f056956379f83aaea4accdb21-450x253.jpg" alt="世贤国小二张交趾陶艺术造型椅揭幕仪式。" width="500" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E8%B4%A4%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md">世贤国小</a>二张交趾陶艺术造型椅揭幕仪式。（李撷璎）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11698194" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/781914ea4b6c19cb24a44a4a2075cbe8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11698194" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/781914ea4b6c19cb24a44a4a2075cbe8-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">贵宾与管乐小鸡造型艺术椅合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>校长何宪昌表示，这是学校第三度加入文化局“嘉义市公立学校师生创作交趾陶装置艺术计划”融入校园的创新活动，结合交趾陶艺师侯吉村老师传统色彩丰富的交趾陶创作，并取材嘉义市在地文化，真正达到发展在地文化的价值。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11698196" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/74eb5bc1f65f4e5a877623d40c16b13f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11698196" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/74eb5bc1f65f4e5a877623d40c16b13f-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">参与制作的小朋友与作品留影。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>何宪昌说，透过设计校园创意造型椅激发学生潜能，提升其问题解决能力，使学生激荡创意发想，借此提升美感素养与创造力，并营造创意活力校园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11698197" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/58e6ede65c906b7573d674a9da7c3147.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11698197" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/58e6ede65c906b7573d674a9da7c3147-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">校长何宪昌(右起)、文化局邓倚欣科长、保福里里长李振煌及家长会副会长四人皆认同作品的特殊性，相当珍惜。 （李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市世贤国小108年度交趾陶创意艺术椅设计比赛，其中得奖作品共41件，透过学校艺文领域教师及交趾陶创作的侯吉村老师共同讨论后，决定参考其中主题比较明显的3件作品，成为2张椅子造型来源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11698198" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/da77d511dc2088812e554b1fceb8531c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11698198" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/da77d511dc2088812e554b1fceb8531c-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小火车有樱花陪伴的交趾陶作品呈立体造型，很受欢迎。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侯承劭同学说阿里山小火车，有着百年历史，它是嘉义珍贵的文化资产，当我们坐上椅子能感受到当时小火车正卖力爬上阿里山，就像我们的人生也是要努力爬上高峰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11698199" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2b23836086b1f586252f2c79bd4cc17c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11698199" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2b23836086b1f586252f2c79bd4cc17c-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">管乐小鸡造型艺术椅。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>喻子芩说日出台湾本作品内容有台湾高山太阳海水和孔雀，以孔雀代表美丽的台湾以高山和太阳表示阿里山的日出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11698201" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ac8bed1125c9a0f832a9c607625e9b34.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11698201" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ac8bed1125c9a0f832a9c607625e9b34-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">去年完成的其中二张艺术造型椅子。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>确认椅子造型后，暑假期间聘请侯吉村指导学生交趾陶创作及上釉，开学后，将该二张椅子固定位置后，侯吉村带领小朋友将烧制完成的小陶片一一黏到椅子的表面，经过一番功夫后，大功告成。加上去年完成的3张，目前校园内有5张艺术造型椅，完全融入校园景观，既是公共艺术创作，也是绝佳的休憩座椅。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11698202" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/22c61c644b01a74e8b894fb1e5731382.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11698202" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/22c61c644b01a74e8b894fb1e5731382-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">也是去年完成的第三张艺术造型椅子。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11698203" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/b338ac000a909be782edde53f06bb7d5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11698203" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/b338ac000a909be782edde53f06bb7d5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">交趾陶艺师侯吉村老师在艺术领域耕耘，很有心得。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11698205" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/c505b6182ee0921c1aaf321c0b25ec8d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11698205" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/c505b6182ee0921c1aaf321c0b25ec8d-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">校长何宪昌致词。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11698207" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/efb09124487383b26a0af0c0fc601d54.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11698207" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/efb09124487383b26a0af0c0fc601d54-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">参与暖场表演的五年级黄志嘉小朋友表现优异。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
