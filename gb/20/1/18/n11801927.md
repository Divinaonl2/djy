<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】黑豆雪耳帆立贝炖汤</h2>
<img width="600" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黑豆雪耳帆立贝炖汤（图/梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><strong>【前言】</strong>年关将至，年货大街出现涌动的人潮，采购南北年货成为应景的首要。干货、零食、腊肠、海鲜&#8230;各式各样的料理好物纷纷上场。在这样冷冷的天气，不妨善用食材，来一碗有干贝的海水咸淡味，再搭配上蜜枣、<a href="https://github.com/iaoxos223/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%B8%E6%A1%83.md">核桃</a>、<a href="https://github.com/iaoxos223/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E8%B1%86.md">黑豆</a>的浓郁香甜，炖汤就在小火中慢慢释放出食材的精华，同时<a href="https://github.com/iaoxos223/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%80%B3.md">雪耳</a>加助浓稠的质地，这样可口的汤品岂有不能拢络家人那挑嘴的胃。这是一道润喉、养肺、养颜、补肾的食谱，享受味蕾也照顾营养。心动了吗？快动手尝试梁厨推荐的“黑豆雪耳帆立贝炖汤”吧！</p>
<h3><strong>【材料】</strong></h3>
<p>干帆立贝10粒（Hotategai 10 pieces）<br />
<a href="https://github.com/iaoxos223/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E8%B1%86.md">黑豆</a>3安士（Black Bean 3 oz）<br />
<a href="https://github.com/iaoxos223/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%B8%E6%A1%83.md">核桃</a>2安士（Walnut 2 oz）<br />
<a href="https://github.com/iaoxos223/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%80%B3.md">雪耳</a>1朵（White fungus 1 piece）<br />
蜜枣4粒（Dates 4 pieces）<br />
陈皮一块（Tangerine 1 piece）<br />
姜3片（Ginger 3 slices）<br />
瘦猪肉10安士（Pork meat 10 oz）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801949" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-15.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11801949 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-15-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">干帆立贝10粒、黑豆3安士、核桃2安士、雪耳1朵（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>【做法】</strong></h3>
<p>1.将黑豆和核桃用清水浸泡两小时。雪耳用水浸泡至软身，剪去底部硬块，再撕成小块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801952" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-10.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11801952 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-10-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黑豆、核桃需要用清水浸泡两小时。雪耳用水浸泡至软身。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.蜜枣和陈皮洗干净后，用水浸泡，猪肉切小块氽烫备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801948" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11801948" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-16-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11801954" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11801954" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-9-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.干帆立贝另外用水浸泡半小时，泡过帆立贝的水也可以用来煲汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801956" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5-5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11801956 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">干帆立贝通常当零食，如果要煲汤，建议先用水浸泡半小时。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.汤煲内放入清水16碗， 把所有材料放入，大火煲滚后，转为小火煲两小时，加盐调味便可以饮用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11801957" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/b6c72bf215c06d01a472b3ab27a9d0e2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11801957" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/b6c72bf215c06d01a472b3ab27a9d0e2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【梁师傅小贴士】</h3>
<figure id="attachment_11801960" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11801960 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?日本北海道干帆立贝通常是作零食用，但因为买多了，所以用来煲汤，干帆立贝的味道比一般干贝淡一些，但鲜味足。<br />
?黑豆（黑大豆）能补肾益阴，健脾利湿，清热解毒。黑大豆善于补肾，常用治肾虚，亦能益肌肤。久服黑大豆，能使肌细肤白。<br />
?核桃味甘且性温，能补肾、补肺，且能有防止细胞老化，并改善记忆力。核桃还可以减少肠道对胆固醇的吸收，对高血压和冠心病者有益。<br />
?雪耳能提升人体免疫力，可治疗慢性支气管炎及慢性原发性心脏病。◇</p>
